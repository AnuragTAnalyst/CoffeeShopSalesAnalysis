# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard ☕

## Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive sales analysis for a coffee shop chain with three locations (Astoria, Hell’s Kitchen, and Lower Manhattan). Using **Advanced Excel (MS 365 Online)**, I transformed transactional data into a dynamic dashboard to identify revenue drivers, peak operational hours, and customer spending patterns.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
* **The Morning Rush:** Discovered that **60% of daily quantity** is ordered between **7:00 AM and 10:00 AM**, identifying the critical window for staffing optimization.
* **Product Performance:** While **Barista Espresso** is the #1 bestseller ($91k+), the **Average Order/Person remains at 1.44**, suggesting an opportunity for beverage-and-bakery bundling to increase basket size.
* **Steady Growth:** Monthly sales showed a consistent upward trajectory, with a **24% growth peak in June** compared to 12% in January.
* **Weekday Stability:** Revenue is highest on **Fridays ($21,701)** and lowest on Saturdays, indicating a customer base primarily driven by office commuters.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
* **Advanced Excel (Microsoft 365):** Primary tool for analysis and visualization.
* **Power Query:** Used for ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) to clean raw timestamps and bucket hours.
* **Pivot Tables & Modeling:** Aggregated 149k+ footfall records to calculate KPIs.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Enabled dynamic filtering by Month and Weekday for stakeholder ad-hoc analysis.
